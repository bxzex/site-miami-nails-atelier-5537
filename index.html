Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make an appointment?

While we do accept walk-ins if availability permits, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to secure your preferred time and technician, as our schedule fills up quickly.

What type of products do you use?

We use only premium, non-toxic, and cruelty-free products from industry-leading brands. Our gels and polishes are selected for their durability, shine, and health benefits for your natural nails.

How long do gel extensions last?

With proper care, our hard gel extensions typically last between 3 to 5 weeks. We recommend coming in for a fill every 3 weeks to maintain the structural integrity and appearance of the nails.

Do you offer group bookings or bridal parties?

Yes! We love hosting bridal parties, birthdays, and corporate events. Please contact us directly via phone to discuss group rates and private studio rentals.
